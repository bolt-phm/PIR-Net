# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

We are committed to a respectful, inclusive, and constructive collaboration environment.

## Expected Behavior

- Be professional and respectful in all project interactions.
- Focus feedback on technical issues and reproducibility evidence.
- Assume good intent and ask clarifying questions before escalation.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, insults, or discriminatory language.
- Repeatedly posting non-reproducible claims without evidence.
- Sharing private data or credentials.
